About Me

I am a lifelong learner, reader and hobbyist photographer. I always yearn to learn more about the world, my hobbies, and everything inbetween. There is no topic that I won't find interesting. I have a large interest in AI, specifically computer vision and genetic programming, as these were my research topics during my graduate degree. I will occasionally look for recent papers in these fields to keep up. However, in recent times, I have been exploring many LLMs and products like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, etc... in daily life and in coding as they provide me an enhanced google to expedite my workflows and general inquiries.

My main hobbies include photography, reading, coffee, and hiking/traveling. I do have a smaller interest in language learning and finance/investing. I have very much enjoyed traveling around Canada in recent years and hiking around exploring nature. I continue to try to improve at photography and learn more about editing. I mainly enjoy reading Russian and Japanese classics, however, will always try to read any recommendations that I receive. I own a Hario V60 and love honing my pour over skills, it's particularly easy in todays age with youtube.
