Education

I am a 2nd year Master's student of Computer Science with a concentration in Computational Intelligence at Brock University. I also completed my Bachelors in Computer Science at Brock. My thesis work explores diversity in Genetic Programming through illuminations algorithms. In order to illustrate this I will be utilizing procedural texture generation.

Hobbies

I started photography as a hobby around 2018 joining the Brock University Photography Club, engaging with much more experienced photographers I found a love for the hobby. I am still quite a novice within the hobby and am always looking to improve when the opportunity arises.

I started doodling in 2008, taking sporadic breaks due to motivation. Up until 2017/18 I would only try to replicate art based on references but have tried to branch out more since then.

Home cooking has become much more of a passion since starting university. Exploring new recipes in recent times is a newfound way to express myself. Primarily italian and asian recipes have taken most of my interest.
